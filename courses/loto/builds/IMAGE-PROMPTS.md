# Image Prompts — LOTO Course (Lockout/Tagout — Control of Hazardous Energy)

## Style Reference

All images generated with **Seedream 4.5** (`bytedance/seedream-4.5` on Replicate).
Aspect ratio: **16:9**

**Core style prompt suffix (append to all prompts):**
> Sequential art panel, industrial safety illustration, high contrast, limited color palette with dark blue shadows and safety orange/yellow accents, graphic novel quality, dramatic lighting, slightly stylized but grounded, no text in image, no faces visible

**Negative prompt (append to all prompts):**
> no faces visible, no text, no logos, no watermarks, not photorealistic, not cartoon, not anime, no gore, no injury, no blood, no graphic content

**Character note:** Workers are faceless by design (hard hats, safety glasses, work gloves obscure identity). Never show faces - allows audience projection and maintains focus on safety procedures rather than individuals.
