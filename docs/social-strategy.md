# Social Media Strategy

## Platform Priority

1. **LinkedIn** (Primary) - Where HR/ops decision makers live
2. **YouTube** (Secondary) - Build authority with how-to content
3. **Instagram** (Tertiary) - Visual tips, behind the scenes, humanize brand

TikTok: Skip for now. Audience skews younger than target buyer.

---

## LinkedIn Strategy

### Company Page: SOPsNobodyReads

**Tagline:** "Turn documents nobody reads into training everyone completes."

**About:**
We help SMBs transform their employee handbooks, SOPs, and compliance documents into professional training modules. No enterprise budgets needed. No 8-week timelines. Just send your docs, and we'll build training your team will actually complete.

### Content Pillars (Post 2-3x per week)

**1. Pain Point Posts (Relatable problems)**
- "Your 47-page employee handbook isn't training. It's a paperweight with good intentions."
- "Signed acknowledgment forms don't prove understanding. They prove people know how to sign their name."
- "If your onboarding is 'read this and let me know if you have questions'... they have questions. They're just not asking."

**2. Quick Tips (Actionable value)**
- "3 signs your SOP is too long for anyone to read"
- "The #1 mistake companies make with compliance training"
- "Why your new hire training isn't sticking (and what to do instead)"

**3. Behind the Scenes (Build trust)**
- "Just finished converting a 50-page safety manual into a 30-minute module. Here's what we learned..."
- "Client feedback that made my day: [screenshot]"
- "The before/after of turning a Wall of Text into actual training"

**4. Social Proof (When you have it)**
- Case study snippets
- Testimonial quotes
- Results metrics

### Posting Schedule

| Day | Content Type |
|-----|--------------|
| Monday | Pain point post |
| Wednesday | Quick tip or how-to |
| Friday | Behind the scenes or case study |

### Engagement Strategy

- Comment on HR/Training related posts
- Connect with 5-10 relevant people weekly
- Respond to all comments within 24 hours
- Share relevant industry news with your take

## YouTube Strategy

### Channel Name: SOPsNobodyReads

**Channel Description:**
Helping businesses turn documents nobody reads into training everyone completes. Tips on employee training, SCORM, compliance, and making onboarding not suck.

### Video Types

**1. Quick Tips (2-5 minutes)** - Most frequent
- "How to Know If Your SOP Needs to Be Training"
- "3 Things Every Onboarding Module Needs"
- "SCORM Explained in 3 Minutes"

**2. Tutorials (5-10 minutes)** - Build authority
- "How to Audit Your Current Training Materials"
- "What to Include in Compliance Training (Checklist)"
- "How to Write Training Content That Sticks"

**3. Case Studies (3-5 minutes)** - Social proof
- "How We Turned a 60-Page Handbook into 3 Training Modules"
- "Before/After: Compliance Training Transformation"

### Production Notes

- Screen share + voiceover is fine to start
- Use Loom or simple recording
- Don't let production quality delay publishing
- Aim for 1 video per week to start

## Metrics to Track

**Monthly:**
- LinkedIn followers
- LinkedIn post impressions
- Website visits from social
- Inquiries mentioning social

**Don't obsess over:**
- Likes (vanity metric)
- Follower count (unless it's actually converting)

**Do care about:**
- Comments and DMs (engagement)
- Profile visits ‚Üí website clicks (intent)
- Inquiries that mention "saw your post" (attribution)
