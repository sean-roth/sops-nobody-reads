## Internal Notes (Not on form)

When reviewing submissions, flag these as higher-value opportunities:
- Compliance/audit mentioned (urgency + recurring need)
- 50+ pages (larger project = higher revenue)
- Multiple training types selected (potential for ongoing work)
- "No LMS" selected (opportunity to recommend + potential affiliate)
- Rush timeline (can charge premium)
- Multiple employees (indicates real company, not tire-kicker)
