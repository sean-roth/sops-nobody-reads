## üéØ How to Use These for Content

### Social Post Ideas

**LinkedIn/Instagram:**
- "Did you know OSHA has 200+ free safety guides? Here's how to turn them into actual training..."
- "5 free resources every HR manager should bookmark"
- "Your food safety training shouldn't be a 47-page PDF. Here's what to do instead."

**YouTube:**
- "I turned this free OSHA guide into a training module - here's how"
- "Best free resources for compliance training (by industry)"
- "Demo: Before/After - PDF to Interactive Training"

### Demo Module Strategy

1. Pick ONE high-value topic (food safety, OSHA, HIPAA)
2. Download the free resource
3. Convert to training module
4. Use as portfolio piece AND social content
5. Offer free preview to prospects in that industry

## üß™ Quality Testing Your Modules

After building a module, test it with Claude:

```
I just completed a training module on [topic]. I'm going to share the content with you.

After reviewing it, please:
1. Identify any concepts that aren't explained clearly
2. Point out jargon that isn't defined
3. Find any logical gaps in the flow
4. Take the quiz and explain your reasoning for each answer
5. Tell me what questions a learner might still have after completing this

Here's the module content:
[paste content]
```

This catches issues before clients do.
